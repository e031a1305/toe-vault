## Chapters Written (Content Summary)

### Chapter 1 — Leo Sun in the 12th House
The paradox of radiant energy living in the most hidden house. Imagery: a lion in a cave before dawn. The 12th House Sun finds purpose through outward giving, not inward scrutiny.

### Chapter 2 — Leo Ascendant
Christopher's experience as a qualified boat captain who has been recognized and trusted by others but has not yet taken command of his own vessel due to fear of being seen and self-judgment.

### Chapter 3 — Virgo Moon in the 2nd House
An inherited inner critic traced to his father. The discovery that the critical voice quiets when Christopher acts from love and is fully present. Connected to the North Node in Pisces as the soul's direction.
