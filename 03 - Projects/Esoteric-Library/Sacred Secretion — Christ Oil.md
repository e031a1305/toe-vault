# Sacred Secretion — Christ Oil / Chrism

## Etymology
- **Chrism** — from Greek *khrisma*, "an anointing"
- **Christ** — from Greek *Khristos*, "the anointed one"
- Same root. Chrism oil IS Christ oil. "Christian" = one who has been anointed.
- Esoteric reversal: the anointing is not external (done by a priest) — it is internal, produced by the body on a lunar cycle.

---

## What Anointing Means
In antiquity, three classes of people were anointed: **kings, priests, and prophets.**
Oil was chosen because it is the substance of light (it burns to give light), of preservation, and of consecration.
To anoint = to set apart. To mark as carrying something now that wasn't there before.

- Hebrew: *Mashiach* = Messiah = the anointed one
- Greek: *Khristos* = Christ = the anointed one

The external ritual (priest pours oil on the head) was always understood in the deeper tradition as a symbol pointing to an internal event. The real anointing is the secretion completing its journey and reaching the crown.

**The question anointing always raises:** *What are you being set apart for?*
Note: the anointing comes *before* the assignment, not after. Clarity follows the experience — sometimes much later. Not knowing yet is part of the pattern.

- The brain produces a subtle substance (linked variously to cerebrospinal fluid, a sacred secretion from the claustrum/choroid plexus)

## The Experience — What It Feels Like
Consistent across traditions (kundalini accounts, Pentecostal testimony, mystical literature, personal accounts):

1. **Anxiety / disorientation first** — the body and ego don't recognize what's happening; it can feel like dissolution or dying; freaking out is a normal response to something the ordinary self has no category for
2. **The warmth** — described as oil, as heat, as light moving through the body; usually top-down or bottom-up depending on the tradition
3. **The peace** — not information, not vision, just presence; the message is always some version of *calm down, relax / I have you / you're safe*

This sequence is the pattern. The anxiety is not a sign something went wrong — it is part of the process.
