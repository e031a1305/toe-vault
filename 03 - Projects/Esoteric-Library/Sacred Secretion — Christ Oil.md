# Sacred Secretion — Christ Oil / Chrism

## Etymology
- **Chrism** — from Greek *khrisma*, "an anointing"
- **Christ** — from Greek *Khristos*, "the anointed one"
- Same root. Chrism oil IS Christ oil. "Christian" = one who has been anointed.
- Esoteric reversal: the anointing is not external (done by a priest) — it is internal, produced by the body on a lunar cycle.

---

## The Process (Esoteric Framework)
- The brain produces a subtle substance (linked variously to cerebrospinal fluid, a sacred secretion from the claustrum/choroid plexus)
- It descends the spine monthly when the Moon transits your natal Sun sign
- Dwells ~2.5 days at the solar plexus / sacral region (the "cross")
- Two outcomes:
  - **Consumed** — spent through base impulses (lust, excess, lower drives)
  - **Raised** — returned up the spine to the pineal and pituitary glands → illumination, spiritual contact, "resurrection"

## The 3-Day Pattern
The ~2.5–3 day dwell time at the "cross" mirrors the same pattern at every other scale:

| Scale | Event | Pattern |
|-------|-------|---------|
| Cosmic | Winter Solstice (Dec 21–25) | Sun stands still 3 days → days lengthen |
| Cosmic | Summer Solstice | Sun stands still at peak → days shorten |
| Mythic | Easter / Resurrection | 3 days in the tomb → risen |
| Mythic | Jonah & the Whale | 3 days in the belly → released |
| Mythic | Osiris | 3 days dead → resurrected |
| Cellular | Sacred Secretion | 2.5 days at solar plexus → rises or is consumed |

**The pattern: descent → stillness → rebirth**
Operates simultaneously at cosmic, calendrical, and cellular scale. Same myth, different frequency.
