## Entry 004 — 2026-05-17
**Tags:** #esoteric-library #ego #absolute #nondual #wave-ocean #solomon #rumi #hollow-bone #separation #calling #book-seed

**The core tension unpacked:**
"I am receiving the power" vs. "I am the power" — both true at different levels.
- Individual self level: the container didn't generate it. You are receiving.
- Absolute level: the divine spark, the universe experiencing itself. No separation at that level.

**The trap:** The ego cannot hold "I am the absolute" without inflating — because the ego IS the mechanism that creates the illusion of separation. It's like a wave claiming to own the ocean.

**Christopher's extension (his own words, better than the original):**
*"The moment a wave starts acting like it owns the ocean, it just falls down and becomes flat again. And then it has to go through its cycle."*
→ This is mechanically accurate, not just poetic. The inflation → collapse → cycle is the pattern.

**Solomon correction (Christopher, accurate):**
Solomon did NOT hold it without breaking. He broke at the end — accumulated, built altars to foreign gods, kingdom split after him. The tradition keeps this in because it IS the warning inside the archetype. The line between good and evil is not a safe place to stand. It requires constant reorientation.

**Christopher's orientation mechanism:**
*"You bend down to lift. You lift from the ground upward. Why not lift your people?"*
→ This is his compass. Not a doctrine. A direction. Downward first, then up. Worth trusting more than any framework.

**Flags:**

The ball came exactly there. Would have gone through the windshield.

**The question Christopher is sitting with:**
*Did I make it happen, or did I foresee it?*
